History

At the outset, when we considered managing the entire bus system in our city, it seemed like a daunting task to maintain all the buses at fixed times. After starting with only 10 buses at the beginning of the year, we have consistently increased our fleet, and now it comprises more than 150 buses.

Planing

Around 2014, we had two buses that were used for local facilities within a 50km radius. Soon after, in the same year, we acquired ten more buses as part of our planning.
